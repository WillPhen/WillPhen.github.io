What and who are the CIS-Insiders?

CIS-Insiders are a passionate group of students in the computer science field led by Dr. Timothy Darr. The CIS-Insiders made this page so that we might interest other potential students into this field to learn all the great things that we have experienced!

Why Oklahoma Baptist University?

As a Christian liberal arts university, OBU is dedicated to the pursuit of raising up the next generation with not only the knowledge and experience young people need to begin their career, but also the virtues and ethics necessary to bring out positive change in the world, both today and into the future.

What does a computer science degree from OBU look like?

One of the major advantages you gain from obtaining a degree in computer science from Oklahoma Baptist University is that you aren't limited to learning a bunch of different coding languages. Instead, you are provided with opportunities to dozens of different skillsets that are all applicable to the wider world of business. With solid foundations in mathematics, applied physics, and business fundamentals, you will come away with not only the knowledge and skills necessary to develop compelling software the solves problems, but also the understanding of how your software impacts an entire organization or community. Graduates from OBU have gone on to fill in dozens of complex business-oriented rolls that were complimented by their technical and creative training during their time at OBU.
